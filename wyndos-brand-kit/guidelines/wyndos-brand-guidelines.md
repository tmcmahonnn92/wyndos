# Wyndos.io — Brand Guidelines

---

## Overview

Wyndos.io is a route management and planning app built for window cleaning professionals. The brand is utility-first — direct, specific, and built for people who work outdoors, not in boardrooms.

---

## Logo

### The Mark
The Wyndos logo is a map pin with three concentric water ripples beneath the tip. The mark works on two levels:
- The **pin** represents route planning and job locations
- The **ripples** nod to water and the window cleaning trade — without being literal about it
- The **hollow circle** inside the pin subtly echoes a window pane

### Usage Rules
- Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the "W" around the logo on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolour the mark
- Use the dark version on dark backgrounds, light version on light backgrounds
- The transparent icon version is available for overlaying on custom backgrounds
- Minimum size: 24px height for the icon, 120px width for the horizontal lockup

### Outdoor Readability
The dark primary background (Midnight) is a deliberate choice — it significantly reduces screen glare when the app is used outdoors in direct sunlight. Signal blue on white text achieves a 4.6:1 contrast ratio, clearing WCAG AA accessibility standards.

## Typography

### Display / Logotype
**Syne 800 (ExtraBold)**
- Used for the wordmark and major headlines
- Letter spacing: -0.03em
- Google Fonts: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Syne

### Headlines
**Syne 700 (Bold)**
- Used for section headers and key UI numbers
- Letter spacing: -0.02em

### Body Copy
**DM Sans 400 (Regular)**
- Used for all descriptive text and UI body
- Line height: 1.6
- Google Fonts: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Sans

### Data / Labels / UI
**DM Mono 400 (Regular)**
- Used for job numbers, timestamps, status labels, and all tabular data
- Letter spacing: 0.08em, uppercase
- Google Fonts: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Mono

## Brand Voice

### Tone: Utility-first
Write like a tradesperson, not a startup. Direct, no filler.

### Never use:
- "Seamless experience"
- "Powerful solution"
- "Intuitive platform"
- Vague benefit claims

### Always use:
- Specific, measurable claims ("Save 40 mins a day")
- Trade language where appropriate ("round", "job", "stop")
- Present tense, active voice

### Feel: Tool, not toy
Wyndos is built for professionals working outdoors. Every word should feel earned and functional.

## App UI Principles

- **Dark by default** — primary UI is dark-themed for outdoor legibility
- **Status colours** — Signal blue for active/done states, muted grey for pending
- **Data density** — show what matters at a glance: job number, address, time, status
- **Monospace for data** — all job numbers, times, and counts use DM Mono
- **Large tap targets** — minimum 44px for all interactive elements (mobile-first)

---

## Don'ts

- Don't use the logo on a busy photographic background without a dark overlay
- Don't use Signal blue as a background colour (it's an accent, not a base)
- Don't add drop shadows or gradients to the logo mark
- Don't use more than two typefaces in any single layout
- Don't use more than three colours in any single UI screen
